My name is Rafael Silva. I am originally from Brazil and I have been living Sweden since 2007. I am social, find it easy to learn new things and I am used to work under pressure. In my spare time I enjoy reading, watching boxing matches, working out and playing soccer.

Throughout my academic and professional years, I have worked with different programming languages, e.g. Java, python, shell script, javascript, and even with different testing tools and frameworks, e.g. Maven, git, Jenkings, JUnit, TestNG, jacoco, jMeter, Robotium, Espresso, Selenium IDE, Selenium WebDriver, Robot Framework, SoapUI, etc.
